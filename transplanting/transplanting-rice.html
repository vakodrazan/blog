Planting-rice

In general, it is almost seventy percent (70%) of residence in Madagascar are farmers. Including Alaotra Mangoro region is one of the most popular of farming rice because there always have a good product every year.

Time of transplanting

People usually deracinate the rice before planting it in the where it should be. They lived it for about two days at least in a good place then it won't dry.

Transplanting rice

After two days or about we can transplant the rice then it will grow.

However, it needs some products like manure to make it better.

Needs for farming

We need zebus to work in the rice field even before transplanting or during the harvest time. They are really useful animals.

We also can use another materiel but zebu is the most useful here in Madagascar, we also can have manure from them and use it for our farming.

However we need to take care of them like put them in a good place, feed them some vegetation after working and give some water.

Harvesting rice

  • Harvesting from the rice field

The majority, it is month of May and June are the time of harvesting rice here in Madagasar. Some of the people harvested rice during the winter time but that is not really common.

  • To keep it safe

Nowadays, people use keet the rice with its stem as we can see in the picture for about a week or so it depends on the owner even though they leave it in the rice field for about a month it won't go off.

People did that because they have a huge rice field then they can't do all the work on one day.

Happy with your products

Most of the time people usually eat or keep their first product but they don't seel it because that is the Malagasy culture "people believed that if you sell your first product before everything, it will be hard to find any crops for the next year."
