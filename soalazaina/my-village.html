My village Soalazaina

Soalazaina village

I would like to tell you more about Soalazaina. Soalazaina is a village which is Ambatondrazaka district. This where I grew up. I can say that people there are so friendly.

About my homevillage

This village is still a rural village but well-known to everyone. It is surrounded by green spaces including pine forests. If people have never been there, it is easy for them to know that Soalazaina is near when they got into a pine forest. Saturday is the market day in there. Also, it is said that most of the people in there are farmers.

What makes it special

There is a company called Bdf in there. It is a Chinese company. It is so popular in Ambatondrazaka district. Lots of people come to visit that company. They went their to search a job. Even people far from the village came there to visit it. They are inquisitive about it.

Bdf company

I would like to tell you about this company. Bdf is a big company which is in Soalazaina. It started in 2014. The majority of the workers there are women, it is about 70 per cent of the workers. They plant something like mushroom but it looks like taro even bigger. They have never told to anyone what they need it for.

If you have questions, you can ask me. Also, if you are curious about this village, I can always help you for that.
